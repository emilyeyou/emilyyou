Hello, I'm Emily!

A UX Designer and Web Developer

Hi, my name is Emily You. I'm currently a junior at the University of Michigan pursuing a dual degree in UX Design and Cognitive Science with a minor in Entrepreneurship. I am interested in exploring the intersectionality between design, technology, and people.

Around campus, I am involved in Alpha Theta Delta, a professional multidiscplinary design fraternity, and Michigan Advertising and Marketing (MAM). Through my experiences in both organizations, I have worked to improve my hard and soft skills with client-facing project work. Creating more ethical and accessible digital interfaces for people is what fuels my passion for design. In between classes, you can find me enjoying the outdoors (unfortunately not in the long Michigan winters though). I love to play volleyball with friends in the Arb, catch a sunset, and explore cute coffee shops and brunch food in downtown Ann Arbor.

As a foodie and adventurer at heart, I have found community through sharing my love for food and exploration, and it has only grown since I moved to college from across the country. This website shows a few of my favorite cuisines—Asian, Mediterranean, and Australian—some of which I have made myself! I also love to travel, whether it be going on a short day trip, weekend getaway, or finding the most Instagrammable spots (yes, I am guilty of this). So, I have shared a few of my favorite places from where I grew up in Northern California grouped by photo-worthy spots, walkable towns, and overnight stays.
